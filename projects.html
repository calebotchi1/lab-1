Motown Studios Database System

I designed a database system for Motown Studios, a theoretical Ghanaian based studio with 5 branches across the country.
The studio needed a system to keep track of all activities inall of its branches. This system would allow studio employees and recording artistes to book studio rooms, instruments, instrumentalists,
sound engineers, etc. It would also allow for people to check availability of certain rooms or engineers to enhance
planning and organisation.
